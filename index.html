Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common escrow questions

Yes, an escrow agreement can typically be cancelled if both parties agree or under specific circumstances outlined in the purchase agreement. However, there may be cancellation fees and the earnest money deposit handling will depend on the terms of the agreement and the reason for cancellation.

The duration funds remain in escrow depends on the type of transaction. For residential sales, it's typically 30-45 days. For commercial transactions, it can be 60-90 days or longer. Refinances are usually shorter, around 30 days.

During escrow, avoid: making large purchases or taking on new debt, changing jobs or income sources, moving large sums of money without documenting the source, making major changes to your credit profile, and failing to respond promptly to requests for documentation.

Our Services

At Top Escrow, we specialize in delivering secure, efficient, and fully compliant escrow services across a wide range of transaction types. As a fully licensed, independently owned company regulated by the California Department of Financial Protection and Innovation (DFPI), we offer white-glove support for every client from individual homebuyers to commercial investors.

Residential Resale

We provide full-service escrow support for residential resale transactions, ensuring a smooth and secure experience for both buyers and sellers.

  • ✓Full escrow management as a neutral third party
  • ✓Preparation and review of all escrow instructions and documents
  • ✓Coordination of preliminary title reports and lien clearance
  • ✓Secure holding and disbursement of funds
  • ✓Proration of taxes, HOA dues, utilities, and rents
  • ✓Title insurance coordination and processing
  • ✓Buyer and seller communication throughout the transaction
  • ✓Notary coordination for in-person or remote signings
  • ✓Recording and final closing services
  • ✓Optional pre-escrow packages for listings (HOA docs, reports, disclosures)

Refinance

Our refinance escrow services are designed for speed, precision, and compliance, supporting homeowners and lenders with seamless closings.

  • ✓Escrow opening and coordination with lenders
  • ✓Loan document handling and borrower communication
  • ✓Payoff demand ordering and existing lien clearance
  • ✓Title updates and insurance coordination
  • ✓Accurate proration of property taxes and insurance
  • ✓Secure wire transfers and disbursement of funds
  • ✓Prompt delivery of final settlement statements and closing packages

New Home Sales

We work closely with homebuilders, developers, and buyers to facilitate smooth closings for newly constructed properties. Our team understands the unique timelines and documentation requirements that come with new developments.

  • ✓Coordination with builders and sales teams
  • ✓Review and processing of purchase contracts and builder addenda
  • ✓Handling of phased development closings and lot releases
  • ✓Title and insurance coordination
  • ✓Management of deposits, incentives, and construction credits
  • ✓HOA setup and documentation
  • ✓Streamlined communication with lender, builder, and buyer
  • ✓Final walkthrough coordination and closing

Mobile & Manufactured Homes

We're experienced in handling escrow for both personal property and real property mobile home transactions, including those located in parks or on owned land.

  • ✓Determining proper recording agency (County Recorder vs. HCD/DMV)
  • ✓Title and registration processing for personal property mobile homes
  • ✓Coordination with park management and HOA (if applicable)
  • ✓Handling of mobile home-specific disclosures and forms
  • ✓Insurance and lender coordination
  • ✓Tax prorations, rent adjustments, and fee disbursements
  • ✓Support for trust or estate-held mobile home sales

Commercial

We manage a variety of commercial transactions from multi-unit investments to office, retail, and industrial properties with the experience and detail needed for complex deals.

  • ✓Customized escrow instructions tailored to property type
  • ✓Due diligence coordination and delivery of required disclosures
  • ✓Environmental report and zoning document handling
  • ✓Entity and multi-party coordination (LLCs, corporations, syndicates)
  • ✓Commercial loan document processing and fund disbursement
  • ✓Proration of CAM fees, leases, and tenant deposits
  • ✓Timely recording and transaction closing

Bulk Sales & Business Transfers

We support the escrow needs of business transactions, whether or not real property is involved, in compliance with California's Bulk Sale Laws and UCC requirements.

  • ✓Bulk Sale escrow setup in accordance with California Commercial Code
  • ✓Coordination with CPAs, brokers, and business attorneys
  • ✓Filing and publication of Notice to Creditors (per statutory timelines)
  • ✓Sales tax, payroll tax, and liability verification
  • ✓Asset, equipment, and inventory transfer documentation
  • ✓Final disbursement after required clearances

Liquor License Transfer

We provide specialized escrow services for transactions involving California liquor licenses, whether as part of a business sale or a standalone license transfer.

  • ✓ABC-compliant escrow handling with conditional release clauses
  • ✓Coordination with California Department of Alcoholic Beverage Control (ABC)
  • ✓Notice to Creditors and publication requirements (if applicable)
  • ✓Transfer of related business assets and goodwill
  • ✓Funds held in escrow until final ABC approval
  • ✓Guidance through regulatory timelines and requirements
